La Trobe University Campus WiFi Guide

EDUROAM WIFI CONNECTION
To connect to eduroam WiFi at La Trobe Bundoora campus:
1. Open your device WiFi settings
2. Select eduroam from available networks
3. Enter your La Trobe University username: studentID@students.latrobe.edu.au
4. Enter your La Trobe University password
5. Accept the security certificate if prompted
6. You should now be connected to eduroam

If you have connection issues contact IT Helpdesk: 03 9479 2222
Or visit IT Services on campus for assistance.

The eduroam network is available across all La Trobe campuses and at other universities worldwide.
